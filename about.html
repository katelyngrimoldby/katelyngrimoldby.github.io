Hi there! I'm Katelyn. If you're on this site: I hope you like my work!

I began my jourvey in web development when I was thirteen. Our middle school class took part in a coding day, and for whatver reason I decided, instead of using one of the 'learn to code' websites my teacher offered us, I decided to open up W3Schools and make a very basic website. Of course, that code is long gone, though I will admit that from memory it was pretty bad. I kept teaching myself HTML and CSS until I was 16, at which point I began learning some fundamental programming and DOM skills in Javascript in my high school CS classes. I was also lucky enough for my computer science teacher to offer me a web design course, where I fortunately lost the poor coding habits I picked up from W3Schools.

Fast forward to nearly a year after graduating high schools with Honours with Distinction, I'm finally working on self-study once again. This time around, MDN and original documentation are my holy books, and while I am rusty, I'm happy to say I'm loving every step of my journey thus far, and will no doubt continue to do so in the future.
